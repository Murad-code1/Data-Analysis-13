# Insight 1:
# Videos with extremely high views do not always achieve the highest engagement rates,
# indicating that audience reach and audience interaction are fundamentally different performance metrics.

# Insight 2:
# News & Politics content demonstrates strong audience interaction levels,
# suggesting that emotionally reactive or discussion-oriented content drives higher engagement behavior.

# Insight 3:
# Music videos dominate overall views but often underperform in engagement rate,
# meaning passive consumption behavior is more common in entertainment-heavy categories.

# Insight 4:
# Videos published earlier in the week, especially Mondays, tend to show lower engagement,
# which may indicate reduced audience activity during work-focused weekdays.

# Insight 5:
# Niche content categories with lower overall exposure but higher engagement rates
# represent strong community-driven audiences with high loyalty potential for creators and advertisers.
